At 100 years old, Korean War Veteran Catherine Alessandri shows how a century of service can shape history and inspire generations that follow. Alessandri, who turns 101 in June, served in the Navy during the Korean War era and stepped into leadership as chief master at arms of a WAVES barracks. She reflects on serving as a woman in a different era with resilience and pride.
